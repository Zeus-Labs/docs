---
title: "Customize"
---

ZeusCloud allows you to configure and customize your setup to fit your needs!

Some ways to customize your setup include
- Activate or deactivate ZeusCloud security rules.
- Mute or unmute alerts for ZeusCloud security rules.
- <a href="../contribute/add-security-rule">Add your own security rule</a> or modify ours.
- <a href="../contribute/add-to-asset-inventory">Add to our asset inventory</a> to include more cloud resources.
- <a href="../contribute/add-compliance-framework">Add your own compliance framework</a>.
